In loving memory of

Julian Wiseman

1964 - 2021

It is with great sadness that we announce the passing of Julian Wiseman on Saturday 24th July 2021. Julian's family would like to extend their condolences to all of those affected by his loss, particularly to all of his students who always meant so much to him. Words cannot express what a kind, caring, compassionate and supportive person Julian was and he will be sorely missed by everybody who had the pleasure of knowing him.
